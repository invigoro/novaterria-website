About the Game

Novateria – The New World is a narrative-driven turn-based RPG inspired by games like Baldur’s Gate 3 and Divinity: Original Sin.

Players begin their journey sailing to a distant colony in the unexplored lands of the Dovlands. Upon arrival, the settlement stands mysteriously abandoned. No bodies. No survivors. Only scattered clues hinting at what might have happened.

As you investigate the disappearance of the colonists, you will explore a vast frontier filled with hidden ruins, hostile creatures, rival expeditions, and ancient forces awakening beneath the wilderness.

The world of Novateria blends a colonial-era aesthetic with high fantasy. Spellcasters wield arcane magic while soldiers carry flintlock pistols and muskets, creating a setting where enchanted artifacts and black-powder firearms coexist.

Recruit companions, complete quests, and engage in tactical turn-based combat against enemies ranging from wild beasts and hostile settlers to undead horrors and supernatural threats.

Companions

Recruit allies from across the old world and the Dovlands, each bringing their own skills, loyalties, and personal goals.

Samuel Rose
🏹 Threcian Tracker

One of the founders of the missing colony, Samuel Rose is a skilled hunter and tracker from Threcia. If anyone understands the dangers of the frontier, it is him. Whether he survived the colony’s disappearance —or what he knows about it—remains a mystery.

Kesegowaase
🔮 Chatot Magician

A respected mage of the Chatot people, Kesegowaase studied both the spiritual magic of his tribe and the arcane traditions of the Allatians. Now he travels the frontier with his daughter so she may learn the same disciplines that shaped his life.

Nature Magic Combustion Magic Arcane Knowledge Support
Oota Dabin
⚔ Chatot Duelist

The daughter of Kesegowaase, Oota Dabin is a fierce fighter who prefers a tomahawk and war club over books and scrolls. She studies magic only to satisfy her father, though her true talent lies on the battlefield.

Isabella Notario
⛪ Soranan Priestess

A priestess of the Zinovan Order from Sorano, Isabella wields powerful magic of light and healing. She claims to have come to the frontier to serve the faithful—but beneath her devotion lies a secret she is determined to keep hidden.

Light Magic Divination Magic Healing Support
Heidi Fuchs
📖 Limish Scholar

More scholar than adventurer, Heidi inherited a mysterious treasure map from her legendary explorer grandfather. The map leads deep into the unknown interior of the Dovlands—but she has yet to find anyone willing to accompany her there.

Arcane Knowledge Diplomacy Song Magic Lore
Tristan LeBeau
🗡 Renan Vagabond

Born of both Renan and native heritage, Tristan somehow found himself serving as a footsoldier for the Renan colonial forces. The disciplined life of a soldier suits him poorly, and he'd owe his loyalty to whomever can help him slip away from it.

Osiyu

A skilled marksman of the Tompiro people, Osiyu is the brother of the Tompiro Sachem. He has taken to the frontier in search of his missing brother, fearing that the Sachem may somehow be connected to the mysterious disappearance of the colonists.
